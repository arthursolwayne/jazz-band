Your quartet:

No pressure.

TIMING: 160 BPM, 4/4 time. 4 bars = 6.0 seconds. Beat = 0.375s, Bar = 1.5s.

- Bars 2-4: Everybody in. You take the melody. Make Wayne hear something he hasn't heard before. Make it sing. Make it feel like a question, like a door opening.

COMPOSITION RULES:

- You: This is your moment. One short motif, make it sing. Start it, leave it hanging. Come back and finish it. No scale runs — that's student shit. Use silence, space, and contrast to make your idea feel urgent.

One key. No modulation. You've got 6 seconds.

Make the intro feel like the beginning of something important — something that has been waiting to be heard. Let the rhythm breathe, let the harmony speak, and let your melody feel like a whisper that won’t be ignored.
